FAQs


When does the project launch?
The registration will be open for a limited time, please ensure you enter promptly. Announcements on the registration closing date and minting will be made on the Unicorn Motorcycle Gang Discord server or by following along @UMGbar on twitter.
What are NFTs?
NFTs are tokens that we can use to represent ownership of unique items. They let us tokenize things like art, collectibles, even real estate. In the UMG case you'll receive collectible digital art, beer design voting rights and distributor discount price. NFTs can only have one official owner at a time, and they're secured by the Ethereum blockchain - no one can modify the record of ownership or copy/paste a new NFT into existence.
What is the Blockchain?
Blockchain is a system of recording information in a way that makes it impossible to change, hack, or cheat the system.
What is an Ethereum Wallet?
Ethereum wallets are applications that let you interact with your Ethereum account. Think of it like an internet banking app, without the bank. Your wallet lets you read your balance, send transactions and connect to applications. Want to learn more? Go to ethereum.org
What wallet is supported by UMG?
Metamask is the supported wallet of the UMG website. Metamask is available as a browser extension and as a mobile app, MetaMask equips you with a key vault, secure login, token wallet, and token exchange. Everything you need to manage your digital assets. Want to learn more? Go to metamask.io
What are the benefits from owning a UMG NFT?
a) If you are of the first Unicornholders you will have a vote on designing a crypto beer.

b) Unicornholders will be the only ones able to buy the UMG-designed beer straight from production.

c) Access and interaction with special sections of the digital UMG Bar.

d) Whitelist privilege on UMG project Roadmap 2.0.
Will the beer actually go into production?
Yes! Small but firm steps. Once the beer is designed, it will be produced in a specialized brewery in Yucatan following the highest quality standards.
Do you have an Authorized Beer Distributor Program?
Unicornholders will be the only ones able to buy the UMG-designed beer straight from production alongside with other goodies of our unicorn holder exclusive store. If you want to taste the beer or distribute it in your business, find a Unicornholder or get your own UMG NFT.
I'm a UMG NFT holder. What can I do with the UMG-designed beer?
Once you get it, it's up to you. You can collect it, use it as a food ingredient (to marinate roast meat for example), trade it for any other good or take it to your friends party; It's up to you! Life is simple: it's about motorcycles, beer, rock, freedom and unicorns.
